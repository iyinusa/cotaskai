Frequently Asked Questions

Is CoTaskAI free to use?

Yes, CoTaskAI is completely free and open-source. We believe in making AI assistance accessible to everyone.

What browsers are supported?

CoTaskAI currently supports Chrome, coming soon on Firefox, and Edge browsers. We're working on adding support for Safari in the near future.

How does CoTaskAI protect my privacy?

CoTaskAI processes information locally when possible. When AI processing is needed, only the specific content you select is sent to the AI service, never your browsing history or personal data. All transmissions are encrypted and we don't store your queries or results.

Does CoTaskAI work on all websites?

Yes, CoTaskAI is designed to work with any website. However, some websites with unusual structures or heavy JavaScript might have limited functionality. We continuously improve compatibility with different websites.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! CoTaskAI is open-source and we welcome contributions. Visit our GitHub repository to learn how you can help improve the extension or report issues.
